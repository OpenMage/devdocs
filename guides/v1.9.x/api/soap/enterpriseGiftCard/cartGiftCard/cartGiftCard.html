Module: Enterprise_GiftCard

Resource: cart_giftcard

Methods:
  • cart_giftcard.list - Retrieve the list of giftcards used in the shopping cart (quote).
  • cart_giftcard.add - Add a giftcard to a shopping cart (quote).
  • cart_giftcard.remove - Remove a giftcard from the shopping cart (quote).
